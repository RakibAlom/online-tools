Best Practices for Effective Letter Sorting

Pre-processing and Text Preparation

Before sorting, prepare your text to ensure meaningful results. Remove or preserve whitespace based on whether spaces are significant content or mere separators. Strip punctuation if focusing on alphabetic analysis, or preserve it for complete character inventory. Normalize Unicode to composed form (NFC) to prevent accent representation issues. Consider whether digits, symbols, and control characters should be included or filtered.

Choosing Appropriate Sort Strategies

Match your sorting method to your analytical goals: Use alphabetical sorting for reference lists, dictionary creation, and visual scanning.
