The delimiter you select fundamentally shapes your output's usability. When you join text with comma, you create CSV-compatible data ready for spreadsheet import. Join text with space produces flowing prose from fragmented notes, perfect for converting bullet points into readable paragraphs.

Pipes (|) work excellently when your content naturally contains commas, preventing delimiter conflicts.
