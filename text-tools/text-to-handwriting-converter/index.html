Best Practices for Authentic Results

To maximize authenticity with our advanced handwriting generator:

1. Vary Content Structure: Real handwriting has inconsistent line lengths and occasional short lines. Avoid perfectly uniform paragraphs.

2. Use Appropriate Rotation: Apply 0.5-2° rotation for natural hand movement simulation. Perfectly straight text appears computer-generated.

3. Match Font to Context: Cursive fonts (Dancing Script, Great Vibes) for personal correspondence; print fonts (Kalam, Patrick Hand) for notes; decorative fonts (Pacifico, Lobster) for headings.

4. Select Appropriate Paper: Ruled paper for educational content, parchment for formal letters, plain for business correspondence, creative textures for artistic projects.

5. Optimize Spacing: Real handwriting typically has tighter letter spacing than typing. Experiment with negative letter spacing for authentic density.
